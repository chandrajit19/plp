Practice Area

Criminal Law

Pinaka Law Partners brings extensive litigation and advisory experience in criminal law, representing individuals, professionals, and corporate clients in sensitive and high-stakes matters. We provide strategic legal guidance from the earliest stage of inquiry and investigation through trial proceedings and appellate remedies.

Our team handles offences under the Indian Penal Code, the Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita, and allied criminal statutes with meticulous preparation and procedural discipline. Whether engaged for robust defense or complainant-side prosecution support, we focus on protecting legal rights, reputation, and long-term interests through clear strategy and effective courtroom representation.

Core Focus Areas

  • Offences under the Indian Penal Code and Bharatiya Nyaya Sanhita
  • FIR strategy, anticipatory bail, and regular bail proceedings
  • Investigation-stage representation and procedural safeguards
  • Trial advocacy, cross-examination, and evidence-led defense
  • Criminal appeals, revisions, and quashing petitions

Investigation & Pre-Trial Strategy

We advise at the earliest stage of criminal exposure, including notice response, police interaction strategy, and risk mitigation to protect client rights before charges advance.

Bail, Trial & Courtroom Representation

Our litigation team delivers focused representation in bail hearings and trial courts with strong pleadings, evidence analysis, and disciplined advocacy tailored to each matter.

Appeals, Revisions & Quashing

We handle appellate and revisional remedies with careful record review, legal strategy, and timely filings to challenge adverse orders and protect client interests effectively.

White-Collar & Corporate Criminal Matters

We support businesses and key managerial personnel in complex criminal disputes involving compliance exposure, commercial allegations, and reputation-sensitive proceedings.

Strategic Criminal Litigation Backed by Precision

Every criminal matter demands urgency, discretion, and detail. Our team combines careful legal analysis with courtroom readiness to deliver dependable representation at every stage.
