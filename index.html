About the Firm

About Pinaka Law Partners

Pinaka Law Partners is built on a simple belief: legal support should be strategic, dependable, and deeply client-focused. We combine strong legal knowledge with a thoughtful understanding of each client's goals, concerns, and priorities.

Our firm offers tailored legal solutions across criminal law, NDPS matters, civil litigation, matrimonial disputes, cyber issues, and corporate advisory. Every matter is approached with care, precision, and commitment, ensuring our clients receive clear guidance and strong representation at every stage.

At Pinaka Law Partners, we aim to do more than solve legal problems. We work to become a trusted partner who stands with you through complexity, protects your interests, and helps you move forward with confidence.

Practice Areas

Our Expertise

At Pinaka Law Partners, we handle a wide spectrum of legal matters with specialized knowledge and practical strategy. Our practice is designed to support individuals, families, and businesses with clear, reliable legal solutions.

Criminal Law

We provide strong legal representation in criminal matters, including investigations, trials, bail, appeals, and defense strategy. Our focus is on protecting rights and ensuring a diligent, well-prepared approach in every case.

NDPS Matters

We advise and represent clients in matters under the NDPS Act with careful attention to legal procedure, compliance, and defense strategy in sensitive and high-stakes cases.

Civil Litigation

Our civil practice includes property disputes, contracts, recovery matters, tenancy issues, and other disputes where practical legal direction and effective representation are essential.

Matrimonial Law

We assist clients with divorce, separation, maintenance, custody, guardianship, and related family matters with sensitivity, discretion, and a firm commitment to fair outcomes.

Cyber Crime

We handle cyber-related legal issues such as online fraud, identity theft, defamation, hacking, and other digital disputes with a modern and informed legal approach.

Corporate Advisory

We support businesses with advisory, compliance, and dispute-related legal solutions that help them operate with clarity and confidence.
